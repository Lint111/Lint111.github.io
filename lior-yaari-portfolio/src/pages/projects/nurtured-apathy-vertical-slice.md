# Nurtured Apathy - Vertical Slice

## Overview
"Nurtured Apathy - Vertical Slice" is a project that explores the themes of emotional detachment and the impact of technology on human relationships. This vertical slice showcases the core mechanics and artistic direction of the game, providing a glimpse into the immersive experience we aim to deliver.

## Features
- **Engaging Narrative**: Dive into a story that challenges perceptions of connection and apathy in a digital age.
- **Unique Art Style**: Experience a visually striking world that enhances the emotional depth of the narrative.
- **Innovative Gameplay Mechanics**: Interact with the environment in ways that reflect the game's themes, encouraging players to reflect on their choices.

## Technologies Used
- **Game Engine**: Unity
- **Programming Languages**: C#, C++
- **Graphics API**: Vulkan
