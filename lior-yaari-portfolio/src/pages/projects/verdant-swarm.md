# Verdant Swarm

**Description:**  
Verdant Swarm is a unique strategy game that immerses players in a vibrant ecosystem where they control a swarm of creatures. The game focuses on resource management, exploration, and tactical decision-making as players navigate through lush environments filled with challenges and opportunities.

**Key Features:**
- Dynamic ecosystem with interactive flora and fauna.
- Engaging resource management mechanics.
- Tactical gameplay that encourages strategic thinking.
- Beautifully crafted visuals powered by Vulkan for stunning graphics.

**Technologies Used:**
- C#
- Unity
- Vulkan
